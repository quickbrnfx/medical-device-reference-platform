## Vision

The Medical Device Reference Platform is an open engineering case study demonstrating how modern connected medical devices are engineered through systems engineering, embedded development, product cybersecurity, human-centered design, and verification engineering.

Rather than focusing solely on implementation, this project documents the engineering decisions that shape the product throughout its lifecycle, from concept and requirements through architecture, implementation, and verification.

---

# Mission

Demonstrate a disciplined engineering process by documenting the decisions, tradeoffs, and evidence involved in developing modern connected medical devices.

---

# Problem Statement

Many engineering portfolios emphasize implementation while overlooking the engineering process that leads to successful products. This project exists to openly document how complex connected medical devices can be engineered through disciplined systems thinking, secure-by-design principles, human-centered design, and evidence-based verification.
