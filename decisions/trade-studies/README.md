# Trade Studies

Trade Studies are structured engineering evaluations used to compare viable alternatives and recommend a preferred solution.

A Trade Study is performed when multiple technically feasible options exist and the engineering rationale should be documented before establishing the engineering baseline.

Trade Studies support objective decision making by documenting assumptions, evaluation criteria, trade-offs, and recommendations.

---

# Engineering Question

> Which engineering alternative provides the best overall solution to the identified problem?

---

# Engineering Outputs

| Output | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Trade Study | Documents alternatives, evaluation criteria, trade-offs, and recommendation. |

---

# Information Flow

```text
Engineering Question
        │
        ▼
Trade Study
        │
        ▼
Architecture Decision Record
```

The Trade Study informs—but does not replace—the Architecture Decision Record.

The Architecture Decision Record captures the accepted decision after considering the Trade Study.

---

# Engineering Principles

A good Trade Study should be:

- Objective
- Repeatable
- Evidence-based
- Traceable
- Decision-oriented
