# Stakeholder Personas

## Overview

Stakeholder Personas are engineering representations of the primary operational stakeholders who interact directly with a system.

Unlike a complete Stakeholder Analysis, which identifies everyone with an interest in the product throughout its lifecycle, personas focus on the stakeholders whose goals drive system behavior and user needs.

Personas provide a consistent reference when making engineering decisions throughout development.

---

## Purpose

Engineering teams benefit from a shared understanding of the people the system is intended to support.

The purpose of Stakeholder Personas is to represent the primary operational stakeholders in a structured and consistent manner, allowing engineering discussions to remain focused on stakeholder goals rather than assumptions.

Personas provide the bridge between Stakeholder Analysis and User Needs.

---

## When They Are Created

Stakeholder Personas are developed during Product Definition after Stakeholder Analysis and Operational Context have been established.

By this stage, the engineering team understands who has an interest in the system and the environment in which it operates. Personas narrow that understanding to the operational stakeholders whose goals directly influence system behavior.

---

## Developing Personas

Developing stakeholder personas typically involves:

1. Reviewing the complete Stakeholder Analysis.
2. Identifying the primary operational stakeholders.
3. Defining each stakeholder's role and responsibilities.
4. Describing the stakeholder's goals.
5. Identifying constraints that influence interaction with the system.
6. Documenting engineering implications relevant to system development.

Personas describe stakeholder roles rather than individual people.

---

## Characteristics of Effective Personas

Effective stakeholder personas should be:

- Focused on operational roles.
- Based on engineering relevance rather than demographics.
- Stable throughout product development.
- Independent of implementation decisions.
- Directly traceable to one or more User Needs.

---

## Relationship to Other Artifacts

Stakeholder Personas refine the results of Stakeholder Analysis and provide the foundation for User Needs.

```text
Stakeholder Analysis
        │
        ▼
Operational Context
        │
        ▼
Stakeholder Personas
        │
        ▼
User Needs
        │
        ▼
System Requirements
```

Stakeholder Analysis identifies everyone with an interest in the system.

Operational Context describes where and how the system operates.

Stakeholder Personas focus on the primary operational stakeholders whose goals define the system.

User Needs capture those goals as solution-independent engineering objectives.
