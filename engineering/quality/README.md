# Quality

## Overview

Engineering Quality defines the characteristics that enable a system to consistently satisfy stakeholder needs, system requirements, and operational expectations throughout its lifecycle.

While Product Definition identifies why a system is needed, Requirements Engineering defines what it must do, and Architecture organizes how it will accomplish those responsibilities, Quality establishes the engineering expectations for how well the system must perform.

Quality is an engineering concern that influences every stage of system development.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of Engineering Quality is to identify, evaluate, and manage the attributes that contribute to a reliable, maintainable, safe, secure, and effective system.

Engineering Quality provides a structured approach for making design decisions, evaluating tradeoffs, and ensuring long-term system performance.

---

## Core Concepts

Engineering Quality within this repository is based on several fundamental concepts.

- Quality is designed into a system rather than inspected into it.
- Quality characteristics influence engineering decisions throughout development.
- Engineering tradeoffs should be intentional and documented.
- Quality objectives should remain traceable to stakeholder needs and system requirements.
- Quality should be evaluated throughout the system lifecycle.

## Relationship to Architecture

Architecture organizes the solution.

Quality evaluates the characteristics of that solution.

```
System Requirements
        │
        ▼
Architecture
        │
        ▼
Engineering Quality
```

Quality considerations often influence architectural decisions and should be considered throughout system design.

---

## Relationship to Verification

Verification produces objective evidence that requirements have been satisfied.

Engineering Quality helps determine which characteristics are important to evaluate and maintain throughout the system lifecycle.

Verification demonstrates compliance.

Quality evaluates engineering excellence.
